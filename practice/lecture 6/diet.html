Weight Gain

A weight gain diet emphasizes calorie-dense, nutrient-rich foods like nuts, dried fruits, whole grains, full-fat dairy, avocados, red meat, and starchy vegetables, focusing on a consistent caloric surplus to gain weight healthily. Combining meals with calorie-rich snacks like smoothies, protein shakes, and full-fat yogurts helps increase intake, while incorporating strength training promotes muscle gain, not just fat.

Weight Lose

A diet for weight loss involves creating a calorie deficit by eating fewer calories than you burn, prioritizing nutrient-dense foods like vegetables, protein, and whole grains, and staying hydrated. Key strategies include focusing on portion control, limiting processed foods and sugary drinks, incorporating physical activity, and practicing mindful eating
