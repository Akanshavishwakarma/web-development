Weight Loss

Days Excerise Details
Monday Cardio Choose moderate to vigorous cardio like brisk walking, jogging, or cycling for at least 30 minutes
Tuesday Full Body Strength Training Perform exercises such as squats, push-ups, lunges, and planks to build muscle
Wednesday Active Recovery Engage in a lighter activity such as yoga, stretching, or a short, easy walk.
Thursday High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) Try a short HIIT workout, alternating between 20 seconds of intense exercise (like mountain climbers) and 10 seconds of rest for a total of 4 minutes.
Friday Lower Body & Core Strength Focus on exercises targeting your glutes and core, such as squats and bicycle crunches.
Saturday Upper Body Strength Perform exercises to strengthen your upper body, like push-ups and shoulder rotations.
Sunday Rest Rest and recover to allow your muscles to repair and rebuild.

Weight Gain

Days Excercise Details
Monday Push (Chest, Triceps, Shoulders) Bench Press: Lying on a bench, press the barbell upwards from your chest.
Overhead Press: Standing or seated, press a barbell or dumbbells overhead.
Dumbbell Pullover: Lie on a bench and lower a dumbbell behind your head to stretch your chest and back.
Tuesday Pull (Back, Biceps) Pull-Ups/Chin-Ups: Hang from a bar and pull your body up until your chin is over the bar.
Dumbbell Rows: Use dumbbells to perform rows for your back and biceps.
Bicep Curls: With dumbbells or a barbell, curl the weight to work your biceps.
Wednesday Lower Body (Quads, Glutes, Hamstrings) Squats: Performing squats with a barbell or your bodyweight is excellent for building lower body muscle.
Leg Extensions/Leg Curls: Use machines to isolate and build your quads and hamstrings.
Weighted Lunges: Step forward and lower your body until both knees are bent at a 90-degree angle.
Thursday Rest or Active Recovery Engage in light activity like walking or stretching to aid in muscle recovery.
Friday Push Focus on a different variation of your push exercises, like incline presses or lateral raises for shoulders.
Satursday Pull Incorporate exercises like lat pulldowns or seated cable rows to target the back muscles.
Sunday Lower Body Incorporate exercises that focus on hamstrings and glutes, such as Romanian deadlifts or glute-ham raises.
